🧢 Getting Started

How To Use CamCap

Start recording your POV in three simple steps.

1

Put SD Card In Cap

Insert your MicroSD card into the hidden slot on the side of the cap.

2

Power On Camera

Press and hold the hidden button to turn on the camera and start recording automatically.

3

Enjoy Hands-Free

Wear the cap and enjoy capturing your journey completely hands-free.

⚡ Video Management

How To Get Your Videos

Convert and download your footage directly to your device.

1

Connect OTG

Connect the SD card to your phone or PC using an OTG connector.

2

Tap Convert

Click "Convert Videos" on our website to open the secure file manager.

3

Select Folder

Browse to your SD card and select the DVR -> Video folder.

4

Download

Enjoy the preview and download your fully converted MP4 videos!
